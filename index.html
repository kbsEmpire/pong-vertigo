HOW TO PLAY
Game Instructions

Objective

Score points by getting the ball past your opponent's paddle. The first player to reach 7 points wins the game.

Controls

Drag your finger anywhere on your side of the screen to move your paddle:

  • Top Player: Drag anywhere on the top half of the screen
  • Bottom Player: Drag anywhere on the bottom half of the screen

The game automatically detects which player is controlling based on touch position.

Game Modes

  • Local Multiplayer: Play against a friend on the same device
  • Player vs AI: Play against the computer at different difficulty levels
  • Online Play: Play against a friend online using a shared room code

Enhanced Features

  • Larger playing field for better gameplay
  • Visual effects when the ball is hit
  • Smooth touch controls optimized for mobile
  • Dynamic ball physics with increasing speed
  • Online multiplayer with WebSocket connection
  • Pause/play and restart functionality

Tips

  • Try to anticipate where the ball will go
  • Position your paddle in the center when waiting
  • Against the AI, watch for patterns in its movement
  • Use quick swipes for faster paddle movements
